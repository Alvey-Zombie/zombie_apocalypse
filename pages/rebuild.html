Restore Water Supply

Water is an extremely essential part of survival, and the reserve you built up during preparation would quickly be diminished. That's why it's very important to send people out to look for streams, rivers, and fresh water lakes. If there's a decent supply somewhat near the place you're staying, it's generally a good idea to make use of whatever you have to build around that supply. Eventually, you could maybe even start moving your supplies to the water if you have enough area and resources for building.

Building large troughs to hold all the water you can is a great way to ensure you and your friends/colony never have a drought.
